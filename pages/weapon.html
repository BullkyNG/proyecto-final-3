Weapon Type List:

  1. Greatswords:

    2. Heavy damaging weapon, slow with great poise damage, not a great moveset

  2. Greataxes:

    3. Heavy damaging weapon, slow with high poise damage and great recovery, good jumping attack

  3. Bows:

    4. Long distance weapon, really efective against casting opponents comes in 3 different types (Long - Medium - Short)

  4. Crossbows:

    5. Long distance weapon, faster than a bow but also less precise

  5. Spears:

    6. Meta when power stancing spears are really fast and do a lot of thrusting damage, they are better when power stanced

  6. Greatspears:

    7. Meta with a greatshield the big boy version of the spear, stronger poise damage, slower moveset

  7. Collosals:

    8. Slowest weapon type in the game, comes with a great damage but a really hard to land moveset
